Magic Cake

Magic cake is one of the tastiest cakes that the world has known.
Few have tried it and lived to tell about it,so these are their stories.

We aslo need to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of consuming magic cake is your responsibilty.
You have been warned and you can view our legal page for more informantion.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for an hour.
  4. Let sit for room tempertaure for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy
